About me

Ever since I was first handed a controller to play games with, I was hooked. Witnessing my thoughts translated onto the screen in real-time was nothing short of magic for the 5 year old me experiencing the joy of playing a video game for the first time. I needed to know how the gently humming plastic box in front of me could produce such an amazingly responsive, vivid and fun experience, and more importantly, I wanted to know how I could make something like it of my own. I've carried this passion and curiosity throughout my entire life, strenghtening it with every new game played and every fact learned. I knew I wanted to make games my career, and so, throughout my life, I have pursued every opportunity to support this goal. Even as I learned more about the more stressful realities of the game dev industry, my desire to contribute to it has done nothing but grow.
